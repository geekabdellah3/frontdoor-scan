Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before ordering your report.

How quickly will I receive my report? +
Most reports are generated within 5–10 minutes after purchase. You'll receive an email with a link to view and download your report as soon as it's ready.
What data sources do you use? +
Official government sources including the EPA, FEMA, USGS, AirNow, Census Bureau, CDC, and state environmental agencies — 15+ federal databases in total.
Can I use this to negotiate my home price or lease? +
Yes. Each report includes a dedicated negotiation section with property-specific talking points you can use with sellers, landlords, or realtors.
Is this useful for renters? +
Yes. Renters benefit just as much — knowing about water, air, or contamination issues helps inform lease decisions and landlord negotiations.
How is this different from a home inspection? +
Home inspections focus on the physical building. Front Door Fax covers environmental hazards inspections miss: water contamination, air quality, soil pollution, Superfund proximity, radon, and flood risk.
What if my address isn't found? +
If we can't find data for your specific address, we search using the nearest available location data and indicate this in your report. If no meaningful data can be found, we offer a full refund.
